FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about Planovar — for customers and vendors alike.

How do I find vendors on Planovar?

+
Download the Planovar app, create a free account, and use the search to filter vendors by category, location, price range, or event type. You can browse portfolios and read verified reviews before reaching out.

Are the vendors on Planovar verified?

+
Yes. Every vendor goes through email, phone, and business profile verification before their listing goes live. All reviews on the platform are tied to confirmed bookings — no fake ratings allowed.

How do I list my business on Planovar?

+
Download the app, sign up as a vendor, complete your business profile, choose your service category and coverage area, and go live. The basic listing is free — premium visibility packages are available for faster growth.

Is Planovar available outside Lagos?

+
Planovar currently serves vendors and customers across Nigeria including Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, Kano, and Enugu. We're expanding to more cities continuously — vendors everywhere can sign up now.

How does the booking process work?

+
Once you find a vendor you like, you can send a booking request directly from their profile. The vendor reviews and responds, you can chat to confirm details, and the booking is logged in your dashboard for both parties.

Is the app free to download?

+
Yes — the Planovar app is completely free for customers. Download it on the App Store or Google Play, create an account, and start browsing vendors immediately with no subscription required.
